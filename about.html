Our Story

Building Trust, One Module at a Time

CoreSwift was built project by project. Every step taught us what Australian builders truly need.

The Beginning Why modular? Why Australia?

Our team saw the same problem repeating: promises during sales, silence during production. Materials swapped. Certification lost. Clients left with buildings that didn't pass inspection.

That frustration became CoreSwift. We built a supplier where compliance is mandatory, not an afterthought.

The Commitment Quality as culture, not checklist

We developed 4-Step Verification because Australian standards don't translate themselves. Every material, every weld, every certification must be verified. We work only with factories that share our philosophy.

When we say it's certified, it's because we verified it ourselves.

The Future Growing with our clients

CoreSwift is young by design.Small enough to care. Agile enough to adapt. Driven toward CodeMark certification and beyond.

Our goal is to be the most trusted, not the biggest.
